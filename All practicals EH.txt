Here are the steps exactly with commands (text only) from Practical 4:

Step 1: Install nmap from https://nmap.org/download and open it.

Step 2: Type scanme.nmap.org to check nmap.

Step 3: Perform ACK scan
COMMAND: nmap -sA -T4 scanme.nmap.org

Step 4: Perform SYN (Stealth) Scan
COMMAND: nmap -p22,113,139 scanme.nmap.org

Step 5: Perform FIN Scan
COMMAND: nmap -sF -T4 scanme.nmap.org

Step 6: Perform NULL Scan
COMMAND: nmap -sN -p 22 scanme.nmap.org

Step 7: Perform XMAS Scan
COMMAND: nmap -sX -T4 scanme.nmap.org
